### Templeton High School

I have been both the Percussion and Visual Captionhead at Templeton High School Marching Band. I coached their percussion program to become the best on the central coast in a single year, and as visual captionhead I brought their visual design and choreography into the 21st century. Making the group competetive in the modern era of the performing arts.
